What is Biodiversity?

Biodiversity refers to the variety of life on Earth — from the smallest microorganisms to the largest mammals. It includes ecosystems, species, and genetic diversity.

Why is it Important?

  • Maintains ecological balance
  • Provides food, medicine, and clean air
  • Supports all forms of life
  • Helps combat climate change
